Summary

"Inception" is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in the art of "extraction" — stealing secrets from within a person's subconscious while they dream. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can successfully perform inception: planting an idea in someone's mind without them realizing it.
